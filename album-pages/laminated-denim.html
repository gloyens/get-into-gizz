Laminated Denim

One of three albums released in October 2022 - the others being Changes and Ice, Death, Planets, Lungs, Mushrooms and Lava - Laminated Denim consists of two epic 15-minute jams - exactly like its predecessor, Made In Timeland (the two albums' titles are also anagrams of one another). Sonically, though, the album is much closer to Ice Death, released just five days previously. Similar to Polygondwanaland, it makes heavy use of polyrhythms, with a constant motorik beat running through.

The vinyl also comes in a denim sleeve!
