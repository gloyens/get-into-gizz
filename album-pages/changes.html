Changes

Changes released on October 28th, 2022, after having been in the works for five years. It's definitely the most considered of the three October releases - the others being Laminated Denim and Ice, Death, Planets, Lungs, Mushrooms and Lava - and it's great. There's so much to say about this album, which combines the complexity of Polygondwanaland, the mellow, jazzy vibe of Sketches, and the cyclical tracklist of Nonagon Infinity into its own gorgeous tapestry of sound, that it would be impossible to fully explore it here. Oh, and the tracklist spells out Changes. Neat!

Jazz? I like jazz!
